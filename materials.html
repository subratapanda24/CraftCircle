Painting Workshops

Acrylic Pour · Watercolor Florals · Galaxy Resin Art
Provided
Acrylic or watercolor paints — full palette
Canvas or watercolor paper (A3)
Brushes — flat, round, fan
Palette cups & mixing trays
Silicone cups (pour sessions)
Resin & pigments (resin sessions)
Apron & gloves

Reference photos or inspiration

Pottery Workshops

Wheel Throwing · Handbuilding · Glaze & Fire
Provided
2 kg stoneware clay per person
Pottery wheel access
Wire, sponge, ribs & loop tools
Kiln firing — 2 weeks post-session
Glazes for one piece
Work apron & towels
Bring Your Own (Optional)
Wooden skewers for texture
Small cutting board for slab work

20+ fragrance oils
Dried botanicals
Dyes and micas
Pouring pitchers & tools

Studio Guidelines
